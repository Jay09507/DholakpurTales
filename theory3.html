Dholakpur Tales

This is a tale on how Kalia lost Chutki

-By Christopher Nolan

Once upon a time in the mighty kingdom of Dholakpur there lived a wicked person named Kalia he used to had a very big crush on one of his friend named Chutki. Chutki knew all this, And she did not wanted to be Kalia's girlfriend. But Kalia was very stubborn person he always wanted Chutki to become his girlfriend. So he decided to do whatever it takes to impress Chutki. He used to give her and her friends chocolates and spend an uncountable amount of money and countless hours of texting and talking with her. More over he ignored his own friends and spent more of his time with Chutki and her friends. Kalia also gained the respect and trust from Chutki's family. He was very confident that after all this efforts Chutki will be with her forever.

But Kalia had also made many mistakes while impressing Chutki. He used Harsh language infront of her and also touched her unappropriately a few times. And due to all this mistakes he lost the respect he gained infront of everyone.

And on the auspicious day of Navratri a Haunting surprise awaited Kalia. Kalia meet Bheem. Bheem was a humble (Mitha) person. Bheem never spent the amount of money or the time which Kalia spent for Chutki.

But still Chutki fell in love with Bheem and they both lived happily. Both of them forget the past which was Kalia and headed to their bright future.

kalia spent the next week crying in corner of his toilet. But still he is in the mood of taking revenge from Bheem. And proving Chutki wrong about her choice of choosing Bheem over him.

Still Kalia is working hard hor his REVENGE...
